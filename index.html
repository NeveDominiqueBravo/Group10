About Aidrix

       

Aidrix is an advanced multifunctional smart glasses system that combines emergency alert systems, health monitoring, and psychological stabilization into a single device. It is equipped with an automated supply delivery system, ensuring that first aid or emergency resources are deployed swiftly when needed.
